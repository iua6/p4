The three major aspects about a watch

"History"

The History of a wrist watch is a plays a articulate role in its significance because that is the watches story and background.

"Quality"

The quality of a watch is very important because the quality reflects on its durability and longetivity. The five qualities that make a good wrist watch is the Weight, the movement, the name and tradition, the Swiss branding and the accuracy.

"Decision Making"

The most crucial process when purchasing a watch. You will be spending a significant amount of cash on a timepiece you'll have most likely own for the rest of your life. You must make the right decision because their is no turning back.
