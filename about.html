About Us

We are Watchology 101

Here at Watchology101, the main objective is to not only educate, inform and advise visitors that are looking to purchase their first watch but also lecture and teach them about the significance of a quality wrist watch . When it comes to buying your first watch, many important factors come into play. You must be ready financially, emotionally and mentally. In my opinion, your first watch is far more significant than any other watch you buy after it. Therefore, that is why my website will guide and direct visitors to purchasing their first watch.
